What if we can only communicate through technology?

With limitations on accessibility, comes the possibility of language as a private enterprise. As Wittgenstein details the importance of public practices for shaping human understandings, the theories of Hannah Arendt expands on these notions. Through her work, she claims action is the highest and most important aspect of the human condition. This human condition entails the realms of human interaction, communication, and the creation of meaningful relationships. It occurs in the public sphere and is characterized by freedom, spontaneity, and uniqueness. We see this action take shape to cover depths of Political life, where individuals come together to engage in meaningful dialogue and decision-making. But what happens when this human interaction is eliminated? "What if we can only communicate through technology?"

Language as a Privacy

In considering this future, there are a few notable outcomes:

  • Dependence on Digital Platforms.

    • All forms of communication, including personal conversations, professional meetings, and social interactions, would take place through digital platforms. Messaging apps, video calls, and other communication tools would become the primary means of interaction.

  • Impact on Social Relationships.

    • The nature of social relationships could be affected. Developing and maintaining personal connections might become more challenging, and there could be a shift in how people form bonds, relying heavily on digital means. Through this, emotional connections may be affected as the human touch and physical presence would be absent. Emotional expressions might need to be adapted to fit the digital medium.

  • Evolutions in Communication Technologies.

    • With the sole reliance on technology for communication, there would likely be rapid advancements in communication technologies. Innovations in real-time translation, virtual reality, and immersive communication experiences could become essential for effective interaction.

  • Creative Expression Through Technology.

    • Artistic expression, storytelling, and creativity would find new avenues in the digital realm. Artists and creators would explore innovative ways to communicate emotions and ideas through technology.

  • Exclusive Decision-Making

    • If these forms of communication and its innovations are the only means in which we can interact, this limits the demographic who becomes unable to acquire such necessary resources. Through this, we can see effects spanning to impact politics as this very demographic becomes silenced in voicing their opinions.
